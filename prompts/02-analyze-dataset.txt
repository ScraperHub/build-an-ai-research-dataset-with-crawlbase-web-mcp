You have a pricing research dataset in Crawlbase Cloud Storage (pages crawled with store=true).

Goal: produce a comparison table across all stored competitors.

Workflow:
1. Call storage_count to confirm how many snapshots exist.
2. Call storage_list (limit 100) to collect RIDs and URLs.
3. Call storage_bulk_get with as=metadata_only first—scan titles/URLs without loading every body.
4. For each competitor that needs detail, call storage_get with as=markdown (by rid or url).
5. Build a markdown table with columns:
   Company | URL | Billing model (per-seat, usage-based, flat, hybrid, unknown) | Plan names | Notable price points | Free tier (yes/no)

Rules:
- Maximum 20 URLs in this analysis.
- If a field is missing on the page, use "unknown"—do not guess prices.
- Ignore cookie banners, nav chrome, and footer boilerplate when extracting plans.
- If storage_get returns Not found, skip that row and note the error.

Deliver the table plus 2–3 sentences on the most common billing patterns you observed.
